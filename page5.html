Medical Cannabis in NYC

The Compassionate Care Act, passed July 2014, officially legalized medical marijuana in New York State. Medical marijuana in New York will be distributed as a highly-controlled product for use by certified patients to alleviate symptoms associated with serious conditions (see Patients & Caregivers for qualifying diseases and symptoms). Excellent additional sources of information on medical marijuana in New York can be found at the Department of Health Website and Compassionate Care NY.
